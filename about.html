🎯 Kan Thiltum (Our Mission)

Tunlaiah online hmanga thil inlei tawnna a hluar tawh hle a, mahse chumi rual chuan inbumna (scam) a tam tial tial bawk. Second Hand Dawr hi heng inbumna laka him taka Mizo ten kan bungraw hluite awlsam taka kan hralh a, kan inlei tawn theih nan a bik taka duan a ni.

Seller in a bungrua him takin a hralh theih nan leh, Buyer in a thil dawn a lungawi loh chuan a pawisa a chan dawn lo tih thlamuang taka a lei theihna tur Platform rintlak siam hi kan tum ber a ni.
