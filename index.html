How It Works

Simple, Safe & Trusted

🛍️

For Buyers

1
Sign Up & Shop
Create an account, log in, and browse our second-hand items.
2
Buyer Protection
Your money is safe. Payments are only released to the seller after you receive the item and confirm your satisfaction, or if no complaints are raised within 48 hours.
3
48-Hour Easy Returns
Not happy? You can return the item within 48 hours of delivery, no questions asked.
Note: Returns are not accepted for physical damage caused after delivery.
🏷️

For Sellers

1
List Your Item
Submit the details and photos of the item you want to sell. Once approved by our admin, your item goes live on the site.
2
Order & Pick-up
When a buyer places an order, our team will come to your location to pick up the item.
3
Inspection & Delivery
During pick-up, our experts will quickly verify the item's condition. If everything matches your description, we handle the delivery straight to the buyer.
🛍️

Bungraw Lei Duh Te Tan

1
Account Siam
Account siam la, log-in in him takin bungraw awm te thlang rawh.
2
Leina Him
I pawisa him takin kan lo vawng tha ang. Bungrua i dawn atanga darkar 48 chhunga i lungawi thu i nemngheh emaw, sawiselna i neih loh chauhvin Seller hnenah pawisa a lut ang.
3
Darkar 48 Chhung Return Theih
I bungraw dawn chu darkar 48 chhungin zawhna awm miah lovin i return thei ang.
Hriattirna: I dawn hnua bungraw tichhe palh (physical damage) erawh return theih a ni lo.
🏷️

Bungraw Hralh Duh Te Tan

1
Item List
I bungraw hralh duh thlalak leh a dinhmun (condition) chiang takin list la, Admin in a approve veleh website-ah a lang ang.
2
Order & Pick-up
I bungrua order a nih veleh, i awmna atangin kan rawn pick up ang.
3
Enchianna & Delivery
Kan rawn pick-up rualin kan expert ten a condition i ziah dan nen a inmil em tih an lo endik anga, harsatna a awm loh chuan a leitu hnenah him takin kan deliver nghal ang.

BUNGRAW HRALHTUTE HRIATTUR (Seller T&C)

1. Platform Fee (Commission): I bungrua hi him tak leh tluang taka hralh a nih hunah, a man i tuk atangin 5% hi platform enkawlna (commission) atan kan cut ang a, a bak 95% zawng chu i bank account emaw UPI ah kan rawn thun ang.

2. Delivery & Pick-up Kalphung: I bungrua leitu an awm veleh, kan team-in i awmna atangin kan rawn pick-up ang. Delivery fee hi Seller-in pek a ngai lo. Delivery fee hi chu bungraw len zawng leh rit zawng azirin a leitu (buyer) in a hmunah an lo pe ang.

3. Bungraw Dinhmun: I thil hralh tur condition leh a chhiatna lai (a awm chuan) thup lovin dik takin i ziak tur a ni. Kan rawn pick-up dawnin kan rawn endik ang a, i thu ziah nen a inmil lo a nih chuan order hi cancel theih a ni.

4. Return Policy & Payout: Bum hmang laka leitu te an him nan, leitu in bungrua a dawn atangin darkar 48 chhung return theihna hun pek an ni. Harsatna a awm lo a, darkar 48 a liam hnuah chauh i pawisa tur hi i hnenah kan rawn transfer chauh dawn a ni.

5. Prohibited Items: Ralthuam, damdawi (drugs), ruihhlo, ramsa leh sorkar in a khap thil reng reng hralh emaw pho chhuah phal a ni lo.
